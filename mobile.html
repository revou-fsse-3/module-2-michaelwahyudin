mypixiv: Spice Up your creating process

Embark on an extraordinary journey with us as we redefine visual storytelling and cultivate a space for unparalleled artistic expression. Since our inception in 2023, mypixiv has been a melting pot of creativity, bringing together artists, creators, and visionaries from every corner of the artistic realm. Join us in pushing the boundaries of imagination and exploring the limitless possibilities of visual art.

Gudako, the visionary founder of the mypixiv art community, laid the cornerstone of our creative haven. In the dynamic world of artistic expression, Gudako has been a driving force since the beginning. What started as a humble initiative has flourished into a thriving community of artists, mangaka, and diverse professionals. With a core team passionately contributing their unique talents, mypixiv stands as a testament to Gudako's vision and the collective success of our creative family.
